Project Brief

Identify narratives/aspects/issues that you want to explore and highlight through visualization of data from any India-specific dataset or datatsets. Identify datasets which when visualized with other datasets offer the greatest potential for an unique, surprising or hitherto unavailable point of view.

You will analyse this data, extract relationships, discover interesting patterns and hopefully communicate an insightful story through an interactive visualization. You will work in small teams (of 2, 3 or 4) to complete this project over the rest of the semester. You will be using d3.js to implement your interactive visualization. Refer to the d3 & data resources below.
